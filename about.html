About Me

My name is Sean Jackson. I am a healthcare worker and career changer. Prior to the pandemic, I bridged to a bachelors of commerce at Ontario Tech university from Durham College to educate myself further and progress my career.

After one semester, we had the lockdown partially through the second semester. Fortunately, I kept myself busy between work and school. I applied into to a business technology management and I was accepted. While I did well in my business courses, my grades were much better in my IT courses. I was naive thinking about a pivoting from a general business degree. The pandemic taught me that this was a complete career change. Last summer, I decided to apply into the Technology Management bachelors of IT. I looking forward to graduating and entering the tech world ASAP.
